The tournament will be held at the Fuxan os Ventos Auditorium in Lugo. To reach the playing hall via Google Maps -> Location

The Fuxan os Ventos Municipal Auditorium is a municipally owned cultural facility located in the city of Lugo.

The building has an approximate constructed area of over 1,500 square meters, distributed across several floors and distinct spaces. It features a main hall with a capacity of around 900 people, as well as auxiliary rooms and complementary-use areas.

The construction features a structure made of reinforced concrete, steel, and glass, with a contemporary-style façade characterized by straight lines and large glazed surfaces.

The interior is organized through wide circulation spaces, including corridors several meters wide that connect the different areas of the building.

The auditorium is designed to host cultural activities and events of various kinds, thanks to its flexible layout and the adaptability of its interior spaces.
